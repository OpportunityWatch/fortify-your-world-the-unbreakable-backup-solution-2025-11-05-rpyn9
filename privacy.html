Cookies and Similar Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our Services. We use both session-based and persistent cookies to enhance your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic functionality of our Services, including authentication and security features.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our Services, allowing us to optimize performance and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to provide a personalized experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your interactions to deliver targeted advertising and measure campaign effectiveness (used only with your consent).

Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies such as:

  • Web beacons and pixel tags for tracking page views and user interactions
  • Local storage technologies for storing preferences and data
  • Analytics tools that track usage patterns and user behavior

Managing Cookies

You have the right to control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies stored on your device
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Disable cookies entirely (though this may affect Service functionality)
  • Enable "Do Not Track" signals (if supported by your browser)

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our Services.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information and backup data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:

  • Military-grade AES-256 encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • End-to-end encryption with zero-knowledge architecture
  • Secure servers in geographically distributed data centers
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

  • Compliance with industry security standards and certifications

We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use our Services at your own risk. Please notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. The retention period may vary depending on the context of the processing and our legal obligations.
