About Fortify

Our story and commitment to data protection

Fortify was founded in 2018 by a team of cybersecurity experts and data engineers who witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of data loss on businesses and individuals. After a major financial services company lost critical data due to inadequate backup systems, our founders realized the market needed a backup solution that prioritized security, reliability, and ease of use above all else. What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted platform protecting over 500,000 users worldwide, handling more than 50 petabytes of critical data across multiple continents.

Today, Fortify operates with a singular mission: to ensure that no one ever loses their important data again. We believe that backup should be invisible, automatic, and absolutely reliable. Our team of 200+ engineers, security specialists, and customer success professionals work around the clock to maintain the highest standards of data protection, compliance, and service reliability. We invest heavily in research and development, staying at the forefront of encryption technology, disaster recovery systems, and compliance frameworks to provide our customers with the most advanced backup solution available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Fortify

How does Fortify protect my data?

Fortify employs military-grade AES-256 encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. We use a zero-knowledge architecture, which means your data is encrypted on your device before it ever reaches our servers. Even our own team cannot access your unencrypted files. Additionally, all backups are replicated across multiple geographic regions to ensure data redundancy and protection against regional outages. Your encryption keys are managed through our secure key management system with industry-leading security practices.

What is your data recovery guarantee?

We guarantee a 99.98% recovery rate backed by our comprehensive Service Level Agreement (SLA). In the unlikely event of data loss due to our infrastructure failure, we provide full compensation as outlined in our SLA terms. Our redundant backup systems across multiple data centers ensure that your data is always recoverable. We've successfully recovered data for over 99,999 customers without a single permanent loss incident. You can test our recovery capabilities anytime using our sandbox environment to verify that your data is secure and recoverable.

Is Fortify compliant with GDPR and HIPAA?

Yes, Fortify is fully compliant with GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 Type II, PCI DSS, and CCPA regulations. We maintain certifications for all major compliance frameworks and undergo regular third-party audits to ensure ongoing compliance. Our platform includes features specifically designed for regulatory adherence, such as granular data residency controls, immutable audit trails, automated compliance reporting, and data retention policies. We provide detailed compliance documentation and can generate audit reports on demand for your regulatory submissions. Our legal and compliance teams continuously monitor regulatory changes to ensure we remain compliant with evolving requirements.

How long does it take to restore my data?

Most data restorations complete within 15 minutes, significantly faster than the industry average of 8+ hours. The exact time depends on the amount of data being restored and your internet connection speed. You can restore individual files, specific folders, or your entire system with a single click. Our granular restore feature allows you to select exactly what you need, minimizing recovery time. For critical systems, we offer expedited recovery options and can prioritize your restoration request. We also provide point-in-time recovery, allowing you to restore your system to any specific moment within your retention period.

What happens if I need to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime with no penalties or long-term contracts. If you cancel, you'll have 30 days to download or restore all your backed-up data before it's permanently removed from our servers. We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied with our service. During your trial period, you can cancel at any time with absolutely no charge. We believe in transparent, customer-friendly policies without hidden fees or forced commitments. Your data always remains yours, and you have complete control over when and how you access it.
